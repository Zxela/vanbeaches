## Mobile Considerations

On mobile (touch devices), the hide button should:
- Always be visible (no hover state)
- Be slightly larger for touch targets (min 44x44px)
- Position may adjust for thumb reach

## Animation Specs

### Hide button fade-in
- Transition: opacity 150ms ease-in-out
- From: opacity-0
- To: opacity-100

### Webcam to Placeholder transition
- No animation needed (instant swap)
- Could add subtle fade if desired (optional)

### Placeholder click feedback
- Brief scale or color change on click
- Transition: 100ms
